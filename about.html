ABOUT NEWRO TOURS

Amidst the challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic and subsequent economic downturn, Sri Lanka has shown resilience and adaptability. With careful guidance and strategic policy implementations, we are witnessing a resurgence, poised to embrace a tourism boom in the coming years.

The indicators are promising; tourist arrivals surpassed projections in 2022 and are expected to double by the end of next year. Bolstered by governmental initiatives, we anticipate attracting over 4 million tourists within the next three years, a significant leap from the 700,000 arrivals recorded in 2022.

At Newro Tours, we stand ready to seize the opportunities presented by this anticipated surge in tourism. With four years of experience in the travel industry, we have established ourselves as a beacon of dedication, quality, and commitment to delivering tailored travel solutions that meet market demands.

WHAT SET US APART

At NEWRO TRAVEL, we maintain close connections with our extensive global network of esteemed suppliers. Each supplier undergoes rigorous screening to ensure they meet our stringent standards of excellence and reliability. Our agents are accessible 24/7 at every destination, providing peace of mind and assurance of support whenever required. Through ongoing negotiations, our dedicated suppliers consistently secure the best prices without compromising on quality.
