Tour of Sri Lanka

Program description

Tour Plans

  • Day 1Colombo - Dambulla

    We will pick you up from Colombo’s International Airport (CMB) and transfer you to Dambulla. On the way, we will visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage and the Dambulla Cave Temple. Afterwards, you will proceed to your hotel in Dambulla where you can relax. Overnight in Dambulla.

    Optional trip
    • Visit Minneriya National Park for a Jeep Safari.
  • Day 2Dambulla - Sigiriya - Kandy

    This morning, we will visit the Sigiriya Fortress, an UNESCO world heritage site. Sigiriya is an ancient palace that is carved into a gigantic rock in the shape and likeness of a Lion. It is surrounded by a landscape of lily-covered moats and water gardens and is famous for its mirror wall of rock. Later, we will proceed to Matale and visit a Spice Garden where you can learn about Sri Lanka grown spices and their use. Once in Kandy, we will take you on a City Tour, seeing the Temple of the Tooth, a Buddhist temple that houses a tooth relic of the Buddha and where you can learn about the life of the Buddha. Later we will watch a Cultural Show. Overnight in Kandy.

  • Day 3Kandy – Nuwara Eliya

    Today, you will visit the Royal Botanical Garden, which was part of the Kandyan Kingdom where you can learn about the flora and fauna housed in this important bio-structure. Later you will see the Ramboda Waterfall, the Temple of Hanuman and visit a working Tea Plantation before arriving in Nuwara Eliya where you could rest and relax and enjoy the cool climate. Nuwara Eliya originally served as holiday home for the British colonial administrators. Overnight in Nuwara Eliya.

    Optional trip
    • Visit the Moon Plains
  • Day 4Nuwara Eliya – World’s End – Nuwara Eliya

    Early morning, we will set off to Horton Plains National Park. On arrival, we will walk to the World’s End Viewpoint for some amazing sightseeing. In the evening, we will go on a tour of the town of Nuwara Eliya visiting the Post Office, the Old Kachcheri and Gregory's Lake Park. Later, we will visit Ashoka Vatika in Sita Eliya, and the Sita Amman Hindu Kovil. The evening could be spent relaxing and enjoying the Cool Climate of the Nuwara Eliya Hills. Overnight in Nuwara Eliya.

  • Day 5Nuwara Eliya – Colombo City

    This morning you will be transferred to Colombo, passing Tea Gardens and Waterfalls, a very picturesque drive down from the hills. Once in Colombo you can relax at your hotel. Overnight in Colombo City.

  • Day 6Colombo City - Departure

    This morning, you will be taken on a City Tour you will see the Gangaramaya Buddhist Temple, the Seetha Malakaya Meditation Center, and the Independence Square Monument. Later, we will take you to a local restaurant for a traditional lunch before bidding you farewell. Finally, you will be transferred to Colombo Airport as your tour comes to an end.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the best time to visit Phuket for this tour?

    The best time to visit Phuket for this tour is during the dry season, which typically lasts from November to April. During these months, the weather is generally sunny with lower chances of rain, providing ideal conditions for island-hopping and outdoor activities.

  • Are the snorkeling and scuba diving activities suitable for beginners?

    Yes, both snorkeling and scuba diving activities are suitable for beginners. Our experienced guides will provide comprehensive instructions and ensure your safety throughout the excursion. Life jackets and necessary equipment will be provided, making the experience enjoyable for participants of all skill levels.

  • What should I wear when visiting temples in Phuket?

    When visiting temples, it is essential to dress modestly and respectfully. Both men and women should cover their shoulders and knees. Loose-fitting, comfortable clothing is recommended, and it's a good idea to carry a sarong or scarf to cover up when needed. Please remove your shoes before entering temple buildings as a sign of respect.

  • Can I extend my stay in Phuket after the tour ends?

    Absolutely! If you wish to extend your stay in Phuket after the tour concludes, we can assist you with making the necessary arrangements. Whether you want to continue relaxing on the beautiful beaches or explore more of Phuket's attractions, our team will be happy to customize your itinerary according to your preferences.

  • Are meals included in the tour package?

    Yes, some meals are included in the tour package. Daily breakfast at the resort and a beachside lunch during the Phi Phi Islands excursion are provided. However, lunches and dinners on other days, as well as any additional snacks or beverages, are not included. This allows you to have the flexibility to explore local restaurants and taste various Thai delicacies during your free time.
